Quranic memorization & Islamic Knowledge.

Darul Azeez is an Islamic Institution aiming to educate and train girls in a safe, secure, peaceful and Islamic atmosphere. We offer programs in Islamic Studies with emphasis on self-reformation coupled with Islamic Education, which then facilitates the understanding of the knowledge of Tafseer, Hadeeth, Fiqh, Arabic Language, Tajweed and Islamic history. At Darul Azeez we not only aim to provide Islamic education but also endeavour too inculcate sound Islamic morals and values into our students. .

Faculty

Supervision and teaching at Darul Azeez are conducted by Jamia Zainab Lil banaat Lusaka Zambia and graduates of Daru-s-salaam and the institutes of Quranic memorization. The staff skilled licensed reciters whose chain of mentors is linked up to the Prophet (peace be upon him).
